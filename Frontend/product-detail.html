Product Description

Paracetamol 500mg tablets are effective pain relievers and fever reducers. This medication is commonly used to treat mild to moderate pain including headache, toothache, muscle aches, and menstrual cramps. It also helps reduce fever associated with common cold and flu.

Each tablet contains 500mg of paracetamol (acetaminophen) as the active ingredient. This pack contains 24 tablets, providing reliable relief when you need it most.

Dosage Instructions

Adults and children 12 years and over:

  • Take 1-2 tablets every 4-6 hours as needed
  • Do not exceed 8 tablets in 24 hours
  • Take with water
  • Do not take for more than 10 days unless directed by a doctor

Children under 12 years: Consult a doctor before use

Possible Side Effects

When taken as directed, paracetamol rarely causes side effects. However, some people may experience:

  • Allergic reactions (rare)
  • Skin rash or itching
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Liver problems (with overdose)

Stop use and consult a doctor if you experience any unusual symptoms.

Important Warnings

  • Do not exceed the recommended dose
  • Do not take with other paracetamol-containing products
  • Consult a doctor if you have liver disease
  • Do not use if you are allergic to paracetamol
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Store at room temperature
